Tim Burton: UFOs

Visual Journey

Bringing Tim Burton's Vision to The Neon Museum

For Tim Burton's exhibition at The Neon Museum in Las Vegas, we were tasked with creating a series of iconic UFO sculptures. The project required careful navigation of museum specifications and a multi-stage fabrication process to bring Burton's unique characters to life.

Our Approach:

  • Contextual Design: Developed pre-visualizations showing the sculptures within the museum's existing environment.
  • Iterative Sculpting: Utilized clay for initial models, allowing for easy revisions to perfect character forms.
